About Us

This courseware was created by third-year Speech-Language Pathology students from Batch 2026 as part of our coursework. Through extensive research, we developed a comprehensive resource on speech-language disorders in adults. Our goal is to advance understanding of these conditions, reflecting the ideals of Cebu Doctors’ University in wellness, integrity, service, and excellence. We hope this courseware showcases our growing expertise in the field.
